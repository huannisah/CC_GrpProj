About CareerForge

CareerForge is a career intelligence platform built for both job seekers and developers. It helps people improve their applications while also turning job market data into useful, anonymised insights for everyone.

Built to Benefit Everyone

Every job description submitted to CareerForge is anonymously analysed for skills, keywords, and hiring signals. On one hand, this helps users tailor their resumes and improve their chances of getting interviews. On the other, it contributes to a growing, public view of what the job market actually looks like.

What is CareerForge?

CareerForge is a Career Intelligence as a Service (CaaS) platform that helps make job applications faster, clearer, and more data-driven, accessible to anyone, anywhere.

The Problem CareerForge Solve

Most resumes never get seen. Over 70% are filtered out by Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) before a human recruiter reviews them. At the same time, professional resume services are expensive and slow, Thus, CareerForge exists to make that kind of insight accessible instantly, and at no cost.

Features

  • ATS Score Evaluation: Get a score for how well your resume matches a job description, along with clear, actionable feedback.
  • Resume Fine-Tuning: Receive an improved, ATS-optimised version of your resume tailored to a specific role.
  • Cover Letter Generation: Generate a personalised cover letter based on your resume and the job description.
  • Job Posting Analysis: Break down a job description into key skills, expectations, and potential red flags.
