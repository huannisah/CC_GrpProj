About CareerForge

Career Intelligence as a Service — built for job seekers and developers alike.

Dual-Utility: Two Birds, One Stone

CareerForge is architected as a dual-utility crowdsourced service. Every job description submitted is anonymously mined for skill and keyword signals — simultaneously helping you land a job and contributing to a public dataset of job market intelligence. No personal data is ever stored. Only anonymised aggregate counts are retained.

What is CareerForge?

CareerForge is a Career Intelligence as a Service (CaaS) platform — a cloud-native, API-first service that transforms the painful, opaque process of job applications into a fast, data-driven experience accessible to anyone, anywhere, via a web UI or a fully documented REST API.

The Problem We Solve

Over 75% of resumes are rejected by Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) before a human ever reads them. Professional resume services cost hundreds of dollars. CareerForge makes expert-level resume intelligence available on demand — free, instant, and programmable.

Architecture

Unlike traditional monolithic resume tools, CareerForge is built as a set of independent, stateless microservices. Each capability is a separate REST endpoint — meaning any developer, HR platform, or career app can integrate individual services without taking on the entire product.

Our Features

  • ATS Score Evaluation — Score your resume against a job description (1.0–10.0 in 0.5 increments) with actionable feedback and colour-coded results.
  • Resume Fine-Tuning — AI-rewritten, ATS-optimised resume tailored to the specific role.
  • Cover Letter Generation — Personalised cover letter grounded in your resume and the job description.
  • Job Posting Analysis — Deep breakdown of required skills, culture signals, and red flags.

Dual Utility in Practice

  • Individual value — users get a better resume and cover letter, for free
  • Collective value — aggregated market intelligence is freely available to all via the Job Market Trends Dashboard

REST API Endpoints

  • POST /api/v1/ats-score — ATS score + feedback
  • POST /api/v1/fine-tune — Optimised resume
  • POST /api/v1/cover-letter — Generated cover letter
  • POST /api/v1/job-analysis — Job posting analysis
  • GET /api/v1/market-trends — Public market intelligence
